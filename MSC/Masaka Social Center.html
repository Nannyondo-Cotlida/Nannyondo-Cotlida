MASAKA SOCIAL CENTER

Masaka social center is a popular recreational and community space in Masaka,Uganda.

LOCATION

Masaka social center is located in;

KIMAANYA/KYABAKUZA DIVISION
MASAKA MUNICIPALITY
CENTRAL UGANDA

Masaka social center is situated nearby to the mosque Ahmadiyya and the school Uganda Martyrs University Masaka branch

Masaka Social center is known for;

Serving as a hub for social ,cultural and sports activities
Offering facilities for events, gatherings, and sports activities especially for the youth.

Masaka Social Center aims at promoting social engagement and provide a safe space for community interaction and youth empowerment.
